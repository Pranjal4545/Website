Introduction

Multimedia combines multiple forms of media, such as text, audio, video, graphics, and animation, to convey information effectively.

Components of Multimedia

  • Text: Basic form of information.
  • Graphics: Images and visual elements.
  • Audio: Sound elements enhancing understanding.
  • Video: Moving images for communication.
  • Animation: Visual effects to explain concepts.

Applications of Multimedia

Field Application
Education Interactive learning materials.
Entertainment Movies, video games, and online content.
Medicine Training simulations and visual aids.
