Information Technology

Information Technology (IT) is the use of computers and software to manage information.

Information Security

Information security refers to protecting data from unauthorized access and data breaches.

Cyber Law

Cyber law governs the legal issues related to the use of the internet and digital communications.

Common Cyber Crimes

  • Hacking
  • Identity theft
  • Spreading malware
  • Phishing attacks

Digital Divide

The digital divide refers to the gap between those who have access to digital technology and those who do not.

Computer Ethics

Computer ethics refers to the moral principles that govern the use of computers and information technology.

Objectives of IT Policy in Nepal

  • Establish IT as a high priority sector.
  • Promote knowledge-based industries.
  • Enhance public information through e-government.
